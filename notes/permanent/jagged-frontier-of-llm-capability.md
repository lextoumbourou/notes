The **Jagged Frontier** is the observation that LLMs can be strong in some tasks while making severe errors in others [@dellacquaNavigatingJaggedTechnological2023a]

A recent paper [LLMs Corrupt Your Documents When You Delegate](../reference/papers/llms-corrupt-your-documents-when-you-delegate.md) showed how through a multi-step backtranslation-inspired approach, they demonstrate that LLMs had severe issues with degradation across all document domains except Python.

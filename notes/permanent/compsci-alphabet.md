In Computer Science, an alphabet is a non-empty set of symbols. Often denoted using $\Sigma$.

Examples:

* $\Sigma = \{0, 1\}$ is the binary alphabet.
* $\Sigma = \{a, b, ..., z\}$ is the alphabet of lowercase letters.

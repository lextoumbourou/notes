For the last few years, I've been a student in [University of London's world class](https://www.london.ac.uk/study) BSc Computer Science program, delivered online through Goldsmiths College.

I'm a CS professional with 15 years of experience, pursuing a CS degree mostly for visa purposes, but I really just want to have the thing done, as I'm not getting any younger.

It costs around ~20-25K USD for the whole thing, spread across 24 modules over roughly 3.5 years while working full-time. I started in October 2022 and am on track to finish in 2026.

The program attracts students from all over the world, many completing it under genuinely difficult circumstances. Something I've come to appreciate a lot more than I expected.

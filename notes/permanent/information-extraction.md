Information extraction pipeline (high-level architecture)

* Text input
* Sentence segmentation
* Tokenisation
* POS tagging
* Named entity recognition
* Chunking (optional)
* Relation extraction
* Output:
    * Structured relations between entities
    * Entity types may include people, organisations, locations, temporal or numerical expressions

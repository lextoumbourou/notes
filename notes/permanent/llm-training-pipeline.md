A typical pipeline for training [Large Language Models](large-language-models.md).

Steps typically include:

- [Pre-training](Pre-training) - learning general language patterns from huge raw datasets.
- Post-training like:
    - [Supervised Fine-Tuning](fine-tuning.md)
    - [Reinforcement Learning (RL)](reinforcement-learning.md) (sometimes [RLHF](reinforcement-learning-from-human-feedback.md))

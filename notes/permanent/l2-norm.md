**L2 norm**, also known as the **Euclidean norm**, is a mathematical concept that measures the length or magnitude of a vector in a Euclidean space.

$$
\begin{equation*}
\|x\|_2 = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n |x_i|^2}
\end{equation*}
$$

A benchmark that assesses LLMs abilities to carry out tasks and manipulate domain documents without introducing errors.

Simulates [Long-Horizon Workflow](long-horizon-workflow.md) document-editing tasks that are typical for knowledge workers.

Contains:
310 work environments across 52 professional domains like coding, crystallography, genealogy and music sheet notation.

Document 15k tokens in length and 5-10 complex editing tasks, which are typical LLM delegated tasks.

Similar benchmarks are domain-focused:

- [CanItEdit](canitedit.md) - focused only on coding
- [NewsEdit](newsedit.md) - focused on news-article edits
